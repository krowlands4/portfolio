Get to know my motivation.

Hi my name is Kenny, I'm a proud father of two wonderful children. At the age of 32 yrs old I decided to completely change my path in life to pursue my dream of being a programmer. I have always been a dreamer of big things and since discovering this passion, it has ignited my personal and unique abilities. When I am away from technology, I am raising two young impressionable children. Leading by example and chasing my dreams gives me the hope that my values will benefit our future generation. I have always been intrigued by technology and the influence that goes along with it, so I decided to make a change and dive deep into the web development world. I've been learning code on my own for the past 7 months with the help of some great online resources such as Free Code Camp, Udemy and Udacity. I have been loving my journey thus far and after completing my first full-stack web developer boot camp, I couldn't wait to start the Advanced Web Developer boot camp. On a daily basis I'm excited to consume new languages, frameworks, and libraries. I can confidently say my personal gifts can serve the needs of your company.
